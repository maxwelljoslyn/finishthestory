Finish the Story

"Finish the Story" is a writing game that anyone can play. This collaborative, online learning experience will help you practice the craft of creative writing.

Grab two people and build your story - together!

Credits

The original board-game version of Finish the Story was developed by Achi Mishra, Chen Ji, Alison Crosby, Kendra Shu, Raghu Mina, and Keyu Lai.
This web adaptation of the board game was implemented by Maxwell Joslyn.
The source code is available at https://github.com/maxwelljoslyn/finishthestory.
